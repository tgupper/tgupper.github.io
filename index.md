## Welcome to my website!



Hello everyone!

My name is Tamara Gupper and I am a PhD researcher in cultural anthropology at Goethe University Frankfurt am Main. 

For my PhD project, I conduct ethnographic research among a team of computer scientists who program humanoid robots to play soccer. Being a computer science student myself, I also actively participate in programming within this team, aiming to understand the human-technology relations at the basis of the development processes taking place. 

On this website, you can find more information on my PhD project, on some of my other projects, as well as on my presentations and publications.
